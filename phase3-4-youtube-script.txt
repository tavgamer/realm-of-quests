REALM OF QUESTS — Phase 3 & 4 YouTube Script
=============================================
Title: "I Added QUESTS, a BOSS FIGHT, and an UNDERWATER CITY to My RPG Game!"
Duration: ~4-5 minutes

---

[INTRO — 0:00-0:20]
(Show the game title screen, then cut to gameplay)

"Last time, I built combat — you could fight goblins and slimes with a sword. But there was no REASON to fight them. No quests, no goals, nothing to do. So today... I added an entire quest system, a hidden underwater city, a BOSS FIGHT, and a bunch of other stuff. Let me show you everything."

---

[SEGMENT 1: THE QUEST SYSTEM — 0:20-1:15]
(Walk up to Village Elder, press E, show dialog box appearing)

"First — quests. Walk up to any NPC and press E, and this dialog box pops up with a typewriter effect. The Village Elder asks you to kill 5 goblins. You can Accept or Decline."

(Click Accept, show enemies spawning)

"The moment you accept, enemies START SPAWNING. They weren't there before — the map was peaceful. Now goblins are appearing all over the place, and they keep coming. Max 6 at a time, new one every 4 seconds."

(Show quest tracker in top-right, kill a few enemies)

"There's a quest tracker up here showing your progress. Kill goblins... 1... 2... 3..."

(Show quest completing, enemies poofing away)

"And when you hit the target — BOOM — all remaining enemies just poof away, and you get DOUBLE the XP and Gold you earned from kills as a bonus reward."

---

[SEGMENT 2: LEVEL UP — 1:15-1:45]
(Show XP bar filling up, then level up happening)

"I also added a proper leveling system. See this blue XP bar? It fills up as you kill enemies. And when it hits the threshold..."

(Level up triggers — golden flash, particles, LEVEL UP text)

"LEVEL UP! Golden flash, particles burst everywhere, full heal, and all your stats go up — more HP, more attack power, faster movement. There are 20 levels total."

---

[SEGMENT 3: TREASURE CHESTS — 1:45-2:15]
(Walk around, find a chest)

"I also hid 12 treasure chests around the map. Some are easy to find near paths, but the best ones are hidden deep in forests or tucked in corners."

(Walk over chest, it opens with sparkle effect)

"Walk over one and it pops open with a gold sparkle effect. The more hidden the chest, the more gold. Easy ones give 5 gold, but the secret ones? 50 gold."

(Open map with M, show chest dots)

"And yes, there's a MAP now. Press M and you can see your position, NPCs, and any chests you haven't found yet."

---

[SEGMENT 4: THE HIDDEN POND — 2:15-2:50]
(Show accepting "The Deep Below" quest from Elder)

"After you finish the goblin and skeleton quests, the Elder tells you about his BROTHER who lives beneath the waters. He says to find a hidden pond... but he doesn't tell you WHERE."

(Walk through the forest, find the pond surrounded by trees)

"You have to explore the map yourself. The pond is hidden behind a wall of trees in the southeast. And since trees are walkable now, you can push through the forest to find it."

(Step into the water, blue fade transition)

"Step into the water and..."

---

[SEGMENT 5: UNDERWATER CITY — 2:50-3:30]
(Fade in to the Underwater City)

"Welcome to the UNDERWATER CITY. Dark blue sea floor, colorful coral walls, ancient ruins everywhere. It's a completely different map — 70 by 50 tiles."

(Walk to the Sea Elder, blue-tinted NPC)

"The Sea Elder — the Village Elder's brother — has his own quests. Sea Serpents and Drowned Pirates are attacking the city."

(Accept a quest, fight sea serpents)

"These enemies are WAY tougher than the goblins. Sea Serpents have 40 HP and hit hard. Drowned Pirates have 55 HP. You need to be leveled up to survive down here."

---

[SEGMENT 6: THE PIRATE KING — 3:30-4:15]
(Show accepting the final quest)

"But the final quest... is a BOSS FIGHT. The Pirate King."

(Boss spawns — huge, red-tinted, with name tag)

"Look at the SIZE of him. He's 1.8x bigger than normal enemies, red-tinted, with 200 HP. He attacks faster, hits for 18 damage, and sees you from across the map. And he's got his pirate crew spawning alongside him."

(Fight the boss, show the big health bar)

"You have to take him down while dodging his minions. One wrong move and you're dead."

(Kill the boss, quest complete)

"AND... THE PIRATE KING IS DEFEATED! 500 XP bonus, 200 Gold. That's the endgame quest right now."

---

[SEGMENT 7: VISUAL UPGRADES — 4:15-4:40]
(Quick montage of the HUD, text, mobile controls)

"I also completely overhauled the visuals. The game runs at 1280x720 now, all the text uses a clean font instead of that pixelated stuff, the HUD has rounded bars with shine effects, and on mobile there's a virtual joystick with dedicated buttons for Attack, Talk, and Map."

(Show mobile landscape prompt, then joystick in action)

---

[OUTRO — 4:40-5:00]
(Back to the title screen or gameplay montage)

"So that's Phase 3 and 4 of Realm of Quests. We went from 'walk around and fight' to a full quest system with 9 quests, 2 areas, a boss fight, treasure chests, a map overlay, level-ups, and mobile support. Next up is Phase 5 — an inventory and shop system where you can buy better weapons and armor."

"Link to play is in the description. If you want to see me build the next phase, smash that subscribe button. Peace."

I added a full quest system, an underwater city, and a boss fight to my browser RPG game! Built with Phaser 3 and JavaScript.

Features added:
- Quest system with NPC dialog
- 9 quests across 2 areas
- Level-up system (20 levels)
- Underwater City (Area 2)
- Pirate King boss fight (200 HP!)
- 12 hidden treasure chests
- Map overlay (press M)
- Mobile virtual joystick controls
- HD resolution + clean UI
