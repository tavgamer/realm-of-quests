"Own multiple weapons? Click EQUIP to switch anytime. Stuff you don't own yet shows greyed out so you know what to save up for."

"Damage Potion — ORANGE explosion, plus 10 attack for 30 seconds. Use it right before a boss fight."

"XP Potion — instant 50 XP."
